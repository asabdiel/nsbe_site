# Site Structure & Content Plan

Reference: NSBE Upstate Zone Board Resource Hub — Website Layout & Build Plan

Pages & Content

- Home Page (Landing)
  - Branding (Build • Connect • Innovate)
  - Quick links to CMR, Chapter Resources, Zone Calendar, Contact
  - Announcements

- Zone Board Directory
  - Photos, roles, emails, office hours, zones served

- Chapter Resources Hub
  - CMR Section (submission link, rubric, exemplars, walkthroughs)
  - Programming Resources (templates, collaboration guide)
  - Finance Resources (budget templates, fundraising cookbook)
  - Membership Resources (toolkit, retention strategies)
  - Communication & Branding (Canva templates, social media guide)
  - Leadership Training (roles, onboarding, parliamentary procedure)

- Zone Calendar
- Documents & Forms Library
- Chapter Directory
- Collaboration Portal
- Zone Board Internal Portal (members-only)

Task Ownership
- Zone Chair: final approval and site governance
- Secretary: documentation & minutes
- Treasurer: finance resources
- Communications: templates & branding
- Programs: event templates & programming resources
- Membership / PD / Academic / Alumni: respective content

Timeline (suggested)
- Week 1: Setup and page scaffolding
- Week 2: Upload essentials
- Week 3: Program content
- Week 4: Testing & Launch

Permissions
- Admins: Zone Chair + Advisors
- Editors: Board members for their sections
- Viewers: chapters

Optional features
- Newsletter integration, analytics, event submission portal, FAQ chatbot
